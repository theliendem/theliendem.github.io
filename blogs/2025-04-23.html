"Don't let the hope of finding a better way prevent you from starting down the best path right now. This day won't come again."

Overview

Welcome to my first blog post! Well, this is exciting. I hope I don't bore you all with my ramblings.

Quote breakdown:

What I take this quote to mean is, don't keep waiting until the perfect moment. Just do whatever it is you want to do now. It reminds me of another quote, something that goes like "The best time to start was years ago, but you missed it. The next best time is now." I like this one because I have a habit of waiting for the "most opportune" moment for something, when really the best time to do it is now.

The second part of the quote is also interesting. "This day won't come again." I got this app on my phone that shows me how much of the year has passed on my lock screen. 2025 is almost a third over. That's insane. It resonates with me because I remember thinking a couple years ago, "why do I need to worry about this college stuff. I've got three more years to go!" But all of a sudden, it's here. It's now. Those three years are gone. Had I gotten started then, I would've been in a much different place than I am now. But again, the best time to start was years ago, but I missed it. The next best time is now.
